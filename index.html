EQing

EQ (or equalization) in music is the process of changing the balance of different frequency components in an audio signal. Our ears can detect a huge range of frequenciesâ€”roughly 20 Hz to 20 kHz. Each element of your mix has energy in different parts of that range.

Mixing

Audio mixing is the process of taking recorded tracks and blending them together using various processes. The goal of mixing is to bring out the best in your multi-track recording by adjusting levels, panning, and time-based audio effects (chorus, reverb, delay).

Mastering

The purpose of mastering is to balance sonic elements of a stereo mix and optimize playback across all systems and media formats. Ultimately, mastering creates a clean and cohesive feeling across all your audio.
